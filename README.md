# Restaurant Management System

## Project Overview
The Restaurant Management System is a comprehensive application designed to help restaurant owners and staff manage their operations efficiently. The system provides functionalities for managing reservations, menus, orders, and payments, streamlining the overall dining experience for customers and the backend operations for staff.

## Features
- **User Management**: Manage staff accounts and roles within the restaurant.
- **Reservations**: Allow customers to book tables in advance, view availability, and manage their reservations.
- **Menu Management**: Create, update, and delete menu items, including categories, descriptions, and pricing.
- **Order Processing**: Enable staff to take customer orders, modify them, and track order status.
- **Payment Integration**: Process payments securely, generating receipts and managing financial records.
- **Reporting and Analytics**: Generate sales reports, customer feedback, and other analytics for better decision-making.

## Usage
After setting up the application, you can:
- Log in as an admin to manage the restaurant operations.
- Navigate to the reservations page to manage bookings.
- Access the menu management section to update the restaurant's offerings.
- View analytics and reports to better understand your restaurant's performance.
